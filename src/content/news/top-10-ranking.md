Gonzaga University's School of Business Administration has been recognized among the top 10 schools to study business in the nation, according to a new report.

The ranking highlights the school's commitment to academic excellence, its AACSB accreditation in both business and accounting, and the strength of its faculty — more than 90% of whom hold a Ph.D. or J.D.

## What Sets Us Apart

The report cited several distinguishing factors:

- A Jesuit educational philosophy that integrates ethics and social responsibility into every course
- Small class sizes that foster mentorship and collaboration
- Experiential learning opportunities including consulting projects with real businesses
- Strong career outcomes for graduates across all programs

"This recognition reflects the dedication of our faculty, staff, and students to the mission of Jesuit business education," said Dean Kenneth S. Anderson.

## Looking Ahead

The school continues to invest in new programs and facilities, including the Hogan Center for Entrepreneurship and the Jack H. Hemmingson Finance Lab, to ensure students are prepared for the evolving business landscape.
