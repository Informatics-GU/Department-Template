The B.B.A. in Business Administration combines a broad Jesuit education with practical learning for business and entrepreneurship in today's global economy.

## Concentrations

Students choose from a variety of concentrations to tailor their degree:

- Finance
- Marketing
- Management
- Management Information Systems (MIS)
- Human Resource Management
- Operations & Supply Chain Management
- International Business

## Program Highlights

- Experiential learning through consulting projects with real businesses
- The Jack H. Hemmingson Finance Lab provides hands-on investment experience
- Global study opportunities through Gonzaga-in-Florence and exchange programs
- AACSB-accredited curriculum ensuring the highest standards of business education

## Career Outcomes

Graduates enter diverse fields including consulting, banking, marketing, project management, entrepreneurship, and corporate leadership.
