The Entrepreneurship & Innovation minor, housed within the Hogan Entrepreneurial Leadership Program, is open to students across all majors and schools at Gonzaga.

## Program Highlights

- Learn to develop business plans, pitch ideas, and launch ventures
- Access to the Hogan Center for Entrepreneurship and its network of mentors
- Annual business plan competition with cash prizes
- Combines with any major to add entrepreneurial skills to your degree

## Who Should Consider This Minor

This minor is ideal for students who want to start their own business, work in startups, lead innovation within established organizations, or bring an entrepreneurial mindset to nonprofit and social enterprise work.
